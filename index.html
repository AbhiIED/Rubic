Rubiks

the cultural club

Notice : Stay Tuned for Upcoming Events

Welcome to the Rubiks Club, a Cultural Club a Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology(MANIT), known for its vibrant and dynamic contributions to campus life. The club serves as a platform for students to explore and showcase their talents in various cultural domains, including music, dance, drama, literature, and fine arts. Rubix aims to foster creativity, camaraderie, and cultural awareness among students, providing them with opportunities to express themselves artistically and collaboratively.

The club organizes a variety of events, workshops, and competitions throughout the academic year, ranging from cultural fests and talent hunts to theatrical performances and art exhibitions. Rubix plays a pivotal role in hosting MANIT's flagship cultural festivals, drawing participation from both within the institute and across the country. It also encourages students to embrace and celebrate the diversity of India's cultural heritage while staying open to global influences.

Through its activities, Rubix not only enriches the campus atmosphere but also nurtures leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills among its members, making it a cornerstone of the cultural landscape at MANIT.
